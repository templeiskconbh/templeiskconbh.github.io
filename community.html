Our Community

Community

Our Diverse Devotee Community

We have a vibrant congregation of over a thousand devotees and well-wishers from different countries such as India, Bangladesh, Nepal and Sri Lanka.

Our community represents multi-lingual backgrounds, with programs conducted in various languages such as Hindi, English, Bengali, Marati, Tamil, Kannada, Telugu, Malayalam, Oriya and Nepali.

With people of all ages, there is a beautiful family atmosphere within the community. The spirit of service lies in the core of our culture, so every individual is encouraged, cared for and involved in this spiritual community.

Our Mission

To Serve Tasty Prasadam

Prasadam means ‘God’s mercy’ and it specifically refers to food that’s been offered to Krishna. Such spiritual food is that is vegetarian and sattvik, is prepared with a very high standard of cleanliness, quality and purified consciousness. Prasadam nourishes both body and soul. During the various festivals, members of the congregation prepare various delicacies, from a variety of cuisines for offering to Lord Krishna and serving to the devotees! More than 7500 plates of prasadam are distributed every month from ISKCON Bahrain.
